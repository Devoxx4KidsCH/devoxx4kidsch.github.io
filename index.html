Devoxx4Kids Ticino - May 5th

During this day we offer the opportunity for girls and boys to experiment and get closer to the world of tencology in a fun way.

Program

The program of the day also includes two meetings dedicated to adults:

  • What changes after the Facebook scandal? The value of data in the new digital society - Alessandro Trivilini

  • Bring the drone on holiday: read and important information - Andrea Perotti

Parents will also have the opportunity to follow with the boys the presentation of "Nothing is impossible" (nothing is impossible) with Valeria Cagnina, from 3.45 pm at the amphitheater hall.

The students will be able to choose the theme they want to learn to know, among the following:

  • Advanced Scratch - Gaspar Torriero
    Learn to play with Scratch, the programming language that allows you to create interactive stories, animations, games, music and works of art.

  • Programming with Minecraft - Lauro Canonica
    Would you like to have a command that creates a castle in Minecraft as you want it? Would you like to have a pack of wolves that protects you as soon as you are attacked by mobs?
    Come to this course and you can program commands that make Minecraft do what you want. At the end of a fun lesson you will be able to connect to our multiplayer server, you can continue your programs and try them with other players even from home.

  • Robotics with Strandbeest - Valeria Cagnina
    We will build and move our robot by the power of the wind. We will learn how a propeller works and how it is possible for a robot to move and walk only with the power of the wind, without any engine. Organization and method are essential for correct assembly. Learning to follow a tutorial is the consequent skill that children will acquire. The boy will bring a working robot home

  • 3D Printing Programming - Salvatore Capuano and Martina
    If you are fascinated by 3D printing, surely you have discovered that it is necessary to have a minimum of skills in creating these objects on the computer, ie modeling them in 3D. In this workshop we will learn how to use a very simple and effective modeling system that allows you to create even complex objects. We will also talk about the actual press and we will see complex objects printed in 3D and we will also see the works done by the guys of the past editions. The only thing left to do now is to bring an idea to realize!

  • The python in the computer! Introduction to programming with Python - Alfio Lazzaro and Andrea Cherubini
    Python is a very common programming language. Thanks to its simplicity and imediatezza, it is also widely used for teaching programming. In our course we will see how to program a small program, in the name of learning while having fun.

  • Playing with data and databases: Wikidata and SPARQL - Ilario Valdelli
    Are you good at quiz games? Do you like solving puzzles? Here you can learn how to interrogate the biggest data source, how a machine thinks to find solutions to puzzles and (if you're good) also like "talking to the database" using their language. This is not just a game, it is above all a challenge

  • Create a videogame: from game design to development in collaboration with Swiss Games Academy - Michel Palucci - Tiziano Leidi - Maurizio Rigamonti
    If you are curious about how a videogame is created the following workshop will allow you to develop a complete level of video games (Arkanoid style).
    In the course you will learn the basics of game design and the development of a videogame with the help of Construct3.

Bienvenue

L’initiative Devoxx4Kids a été lancée en Belgique en 2012 : celle-ci consiste à organiser, une fois par an, une journée d’ateliers d’initiation à la programmation et de découverte de l’informatique en général pour les enfants. Une sorte de conférence de geeks pour les mini pouces, avec des robots, du code et beaucoup de fun et de bonbons!
